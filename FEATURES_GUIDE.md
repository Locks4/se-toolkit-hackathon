# 🎉 New Features Guide

## What's New

Your Goal Tracker now has **powerful new features** to help you track your goals even better!

---

## 🗓️ 1. Date Navigation & Day View

### Navigate Through Days
- **Click any day on the calendar** to see what goals you had for that day
- Use **← → arrows** in day view to browse consecutive days
- Click **📍 Today** button to jump back to today's goals

### What You'll See
When viewing a specific day, you'll see:
- All goals for that day
- Progress bar showing completion percentage
- Which goals were completed (marked with ✓)
- Subgoals for each goal (expandable)

### Perfect For
- Reviewing your past performance
- Planning future goals
- Tracking progress over time

## 📋 2. SubGoals - Break Down Big Goals

### What Are SubGoals?
SubGoals are smaller tasks that make up a larger goal. They help you track progress on complex goals.

**Example:**
- **Main Goal:** "Exercise"
  - SubGoal 1: "10 minutes cardio" ✓
  - SubGoal 2: "20 push-ups" ✓
  - SubGoal 3: "Stretch for 5 minutes" ⬜

### How to Add SubGoals
1. Find your goal in the **Day View** (click any calendar day)
2. **Click on the goal** to expand it
3. Click **"+ Add Subgoal"**
4. Type the subgoal name and press Enter or click "Add"

### How to Use SubGoals
- **Check off** subgoals as you complete them
- **Uncheck** if you need to redo them
- **Delete** subgoals you no longer need (🗑️ icon)
- Track progress: Shows "X/Y subgoals completed"

### Benefits
✅ See detailed progress on complex goals
✅ Stay motivated by checking off small wins
✅ Never lose track of what you've done today

## 📅 3. Scheduled Goals - One-Time Future Goals

### What Are Scheduled Goals?
Unlike daily recurring goals, scheduled goals appear only on **specific dates** you choose.

**Use Cases:**
- "Dentist appointment" on April 15th
- "Submit project" on April 20th
- "Birthday party preparation" on April 25th

### How to Schedule a Goal
1. Click **"+ Add Goal"**
2. Enter the goal title and description
3. Select **"One-time (scheduled)"** option
4. **Pick a date** from the date picker
5. Click **"Add Goal"**

### Goal Types
- **🔄 Daily (recurring):** Shows every day until deleted
  - Examples: "Exercise", "Read 30 minutes", "Meditate"
  
- **📅 One-time (scheduled):** Shows only on the scheduled date
  - Examples: "Doctor appointment", "Presentation", "Party"

### Visual Indicators
- **Daily goals:** Show blue "Daily" badge
- **Scheduled goals:** Show orange date badge
